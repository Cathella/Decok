Straight shaped Kitchen

This single wall design of a modular kitchen is the ideal for a home that is looking to save on space. Leaving no room for wastage, this design gives you the space for storage as well as your electrical kitchen gadgets. The closed shelves placed overhead and below make straight kitchen design a jam-free one and a joy to utilize.

U-Shaped Kitchen

The U-shaped kitchen elevates the kitchen functionality beyond the standard working Triangle. It identifies the 5 main kitchen activities into zones (consumable, non-consumable, cleaning, preparation, and cooking) and places them in relation with other zones. This optimizes workflow movement during the performance of tasks and increases storage space.

Love this Design?

L-Shaped Kitchen

The L-shaped kitchen is ideal for accomplishing your daily tasks while also being an attractive set of design making your kitchen a delight to watch. This one is made out of best quality raw materials it's accompanied with refined set of accessories including the right cabinetry. We understand that it's not just beauty everyone looks out for but also sufficient storage space as well. With delightfully crafted series of cabinets and drawers, you will not be falling short of storage space.

Parallel shaped Kitchen

The kitchen is the hub of any home; therefore, it is important that a kitchen is well planned, adequately organized and that it maximizes all the usable storage space to perform the kitchen tasks successfully. This design seeks to dramatically shorten the distance travelled and movements in the kitchen during tasks. In this design we have simplified the workflow by correctly allocating all kitchen items in their respective zones.

Love this Design?

L-Shaped Kitchen

This L-shaped kitchen utilizes just enough wall space to help you add in a small area for dining if you please. A mix of closed and open cabinetry overhead and a closed set of shelves below give you ample storage space. This kitchen design ensures that your preparation and cooking spaces are well segregated to maintain cleanliness and hygiene.

Love this Design?

U-Shaped Kitchen

This U-shaped kitchen seeks to customise your kitchen to your needs, not just your floor space. It is a perfect example of a functional and ergonomic kitchen design where our kitchen designers streamlined the workflow movement into the 5 zones within the kitchen - Consumable, Non-Consumable, Cleaning, Preparation and Cooking.
